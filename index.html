Operational model equations
General form (Hill slope n)
Response (E)
E = [A]n  ·  τn  ·  Em [A]n  ·  τn  +  ([A] + KA)n
Potency (EC50)
EC50 = KA (2 + τn)1/n − 1
Maximal response (Emax)
Emax = τn  ·  Em τn + 1
Simplified form (when n = 1)
Response (E)
E = [A]  ·  τ  ·  Em [A] (τ + 1) + KA
Potency (EC50)
EC50 = KA τ + 1
Maximal response (Emax)
Emax = τ  ·  Em τ + 1
Parameter definitions [A] = agonist concentration  ·  τ = operational efficacy (incorporates receptor density)  ·  KA = functional affinity  ·  Em = maximum possible system response  ·  n = transducer (Hill) slope
